Our Services

Because we care about you, we have a full staff of licensed and trained professionals here to support YOU in your times of need.

Counseling Services

All counseling services are provided by licensed trained professionals who are here to provide 1:1 and family counseling.

Peer Support Services

All of our Peer Support Services are group sessions with others who have faced some of the
same challenges as you and are here to support and assist you with overcoming those challenges.

Miscellaneous Services

We offer a variety of miscellaneous services that include:

  • Dental Cleanings
  • Haircuts
  • Giveaways such as hygiene products, new and used clothing donated by the community, and more!
