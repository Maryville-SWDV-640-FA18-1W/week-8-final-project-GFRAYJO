About Us

We are a network of individuals working together to help strengthen our community in the time of need. We work united to help provide you with a quality of life. Some of the services we offer include: Indiviual and Family Counseling, Peer-Support services, assistance with housing, food, provisional services, personal hygience services, and assistance with healthcare/medical resources. All of our services are offered at little to no cost to you. Our services are open to everyone in the community.

Mission:

Our mission is to serve our community in the time of need by helping you take one step at a time.
